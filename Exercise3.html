How to cook a Chicken Tinola

Dish: Chicken Tinola (Good for 6 servings)

A.) Materials to be Used

  • Casserole
  • Pot
  • Bowl
  • Mineral Water
  • Knife
  • Spatula
  • Stove/cooker
  • Ladle

B.) Ingredients

  1. 0.5 kg - Chicken Thigh and Leg
  2. 2 sachets - MAGGI Magic Sarap 8g
  3. 4 cloves - Garlic
  4. 1 pc - Onion
  5. 2 pcs - Ginger
  6. 1 stalk - Lemongrass
  7. 1 pc - Finger Chili
  8. 2 tbsp - Vegetable Oil
  9. 4 cups - Water
  10. 1 pc - Papaya
  11. Hot Green Peppers

C.) Procedure and steps on how to cook Chicken Tinola

  1. Season chicken with 1 sachet of the MAGGI Magic Sarap
  2. Saute garlic, onion, ginger, lemongrass and finger chili in oil
  3. Add chicken and cook for 5 minutes
  4. Pour water, boil, skim the scum and simmer for 10 Minutes (maximum)
  5. Add papaya and simmer for another 10 minutes (maximum)
  6. Season with MAGGI Magic Sarap, Stir in chili tops
  7. Transfer into a serving bowl and serve

NOTE: For better and deliciously cooked chicken tinola

  • The whole process would take for about approximately of 25 minutes duration.
  • For better and deliciously made dish is that the chicken must be cut in uniform size to truly ensure even cooking.
  • In order to achieve clear and cloud-free broth is that you must remove amy excess scum that continually floats on the top of the dish.

Have fun eating! Enjoy and have a blast!
